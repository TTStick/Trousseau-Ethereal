The Curator's Manifesto

I. The Purpose

In an age of ephemeral data, The Ethereal Trousseau serves as a permanent digital sanctuary for textile artistry. It is designed to catalog, preserve, and display the silhouette of garments, stripping away the noise of the physical world to reveal the essence of the design.

II. The Mechanism

This archive utilizes local neural processing. Upon the acquisition of a new image (Capture), the system autonomously isolates the subject from its surroundings (Segmentation), centers the geometry, and mounts it upon a standardized parchment canvas. No data leaves this local environment; privacy is absolute.

III. Usage Instructions

To Archive: Select "ADD" from the navigation ribbon. Choose a photograph. The automated tailor will process the garment instantly.
To Observe: Browse the main gallery. Select any specimen to view the Curator's detailed remarks.
